Research & Publications

arXiv · January 2026

Leveraging LLMs for Turkish Skill Extraction

Kariyer.net R&D Center · Istanbul Technical University

Introduces the first Turkish skill extraction dataset with 4,819 labeled skill spans from 327 job postings. Evaluates large language models with dynamic prompting strategies — the best pipeline using Claude Sonnet with dynamic few-shot prompting and GPT-4o reranking achieves an end-to-end score of 0.56 on ESCO taxonomy linking in a low-resource setting. Demonstrates that LLMs can substantially improve skill extraction performance beyond classical supervised methods.
