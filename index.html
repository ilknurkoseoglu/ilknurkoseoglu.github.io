About

From particle tracks to agentic systems

I'm an R&D Program Manager and Certified GenAI Leader with 10+ years bridging frontier scientific research and enterprise R&D commercialization — from detector physics at CERN, Fermilab and GSI to leading AI transformation at Kariyer.net. I'm an author on 40+ peer-reviewed papers spanning CMS/LHC, the PANDA detector and Belle II — and, since my M.Sc. on deep learning for supersymmetry, machine learning.

In 1.5 years I lifted corporate R&D output roughly 6× — from 3 publications and 1 patent filing to 17 paper submissions and 7 patent applications (IEEE, ACM), including a demo selected among only 45 worldwide alongside Meta and UC Berkeley. I run TÜBİTAK programs end to end, build university–industry consortiums, and stay hands-on: designing and coding agentic AI, multi-agent and RAG systems in Python and C++.

I manage the full lifecycle — proposal, budget, timeline, risk, and technical/financial reporting — and I'm comfortable being the public face of corporate R&D at international conferences and consortiums.
